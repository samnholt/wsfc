ESTABLISHED 2020

Following a succesful debut season in the Rochdale, Bury, and District Sunday Football League Division Four, WSFC look forward to taking on teams in Division Two.

Formed during the depths of the global pandemic, we have thouroughly enjoyed the challenge so far and found it a pleasure to compete in the RBDSFL.

We play our home matches at Matthew Moss High School in Rochdale. We are proudly sponsored by DT Law Butchers, Altered Notion, and 01706.

Rochdale Heritage

We are based at Woolworths Sports and Social Club in Castleton, which was built for the workers of the former shopping chain. So yes, like the shop!

A succesful debut

We finished in second place in a close fought battle for Division 4 in 2020/21. We hope to go one better in 2021/22.
